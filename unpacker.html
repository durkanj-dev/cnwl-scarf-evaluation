SCARF Survey — Response Unpacker

Paste your raw response data from Google Sheets below to view responses in a readable table.

How to use:
  1. Go to your Google Sheet: Open Sheet
  2. Click on the Form responses 1 tab
  3. Click column B header to select all JSON data
  4. Press Ctrl+C to copy
  5. Paste into the box below and click Unpack responses
